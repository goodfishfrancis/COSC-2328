Starting with the Seiko SKX171

For our 3 year anniversary, my girlfriend bought for me, a Seiko SKX171 professional dive watch. I'm not a professional diver, but I've always loved the timeless aesthetic and practicality of dive watches.

My first mod: Installing an SKX007 Bezel

The first modification I made to the watch was switching out the stock bezel for a Seiko SKX007 bezel. This is a rather easy modification because the SKX171 and SKX007 use the same watch case. It's simply a matter of popping the bezel off and putting the new one on.

Overhauling the Watch's Aesthetic

After doing the first mod, I thirsted for more. I wanted to completely overhaul the aesthetic of my watch. I was aiming for a Rolex Submariner homage. This would include switching the stock hardlex crystal for a domed blue-anti-reflective coated sapphire crystal and installing yet another new Rolex Submariner style bezel and ceramic bezel insert.
